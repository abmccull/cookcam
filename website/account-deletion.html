How to Delete Your CookCam AI Account

You have two convenient options to request account deletion:

üì± Option 1: Delete Through the App (Recommended)

  1. Open the CookCam AI app on your mobile device
  2. Navigate to Profile/Settings by tapping your profile icon
  3. Scroll down to "Account Settings" section
  4. Tap "Delete Account" option
  5. Confirm your password when prompted for security verification
  6. Review the deletion information and confirm your choice
  7. Your account will be immediately deactivated and data deletion will begin

üìß Option 2: Email Support Request

  1. Send an email to our support team at support@cookcam.ai
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include the following information:
    • Your registered email address
    • Your CookCam AI username (if different from email)
    • Confirmation that you want to permanently delete your account
  4. We will respond within 2 business days to verify your identity
  5. Once verified, account deletion will be processed within 30 days
