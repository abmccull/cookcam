Privacy Policy

How CookCam AI collects, uses, and protects your information

Effective Date: December 1, 2024
Last Updated: December 1, 2024

1. Who We Are

ABM Studios LLC ("ABM," "we," "our," "us") operates the CookCam AI mobile application and related websites, communications, and services (collectively, the "Services"). We are registered in the United States at 30 North Gould Street, Sheridan, WY 82801.

2. Scope

This policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information about individuals who:

  • download, install, or use the CookCam AI app;
  • visit our websites or contact our support team;
  • participate in the CookCam AI Creator program.

5. How We Use Information

  • Run, maintain, and improve ingredient recognition, recipe generation, and all other app features.
  • Personalize recipe recommendations, tips, and in-app content.
  • Monitor performance, detect fraud, and fix bugs.
  • Send transactional messages (account, subscription, security).
  • Send optional product updates, promotions, or newsletters if you opt in.
  • For Creator tier: calculate commissions, display analytics, and remit payouts.

Important: No content you provide is used to train our own or third-party AI systems beyond delivering the requested recipe or prediction.

We do not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioural advertising. California residents can exercise "Do Not Sell or Share" rights via in-app controls.

7. International Transfers

We store data in the United States. If you are outside the US, data may be transferred to US servers. We rely on Standard Contractual Clauses and equivalent safeguards for EEA/UK/Swiss transfers. A copy of these clauses is available on request.

8. Security Measures

  • TLS 1.2+ for all data in transit.
  • AES-256 encryption at rest.
  • Role-based access; least-privilege principle.
  • Annual penetration tests; quarterly access reviews.
  • 24-hour image auto-deletion job.
  • Incident response plan that meets GDPR/CPRA breach-notice timelines.

Submit requests in-app under Settings ‚Üí Privacy Center or email privacy@cookcam.ai. We verify identity via signed-in session or two-factor email check and respond within 30 days (45 days in CA).

10. Retention Schedule

  • Active account: keep data only as needed for stated purposes.
  • Deleted account: erase personal data within 30 days except records we must keep for legal or security reasons (max 7 years).
  • Anonymised, aggregated analytics kept indefinitely.

11. Children

The Services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13. Parents who believe their child has provided personal data may contact us for immediate erasure.

12. Automated Decision-Making & Profiling

We use automated algorithms to suggest recipes and personalise content. These decisions produce no legal or similarly significant effects. You may request human review by contacting support.

13. Changes to This Policy

We post any material changes in-app and update the "Last Updated" date. Continuing to use the Services after the effective date means you accept the revised policy.
