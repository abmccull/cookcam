How accurate is the ingredient scanning?

Our AI achieves 98%+ accuracy in ingredient recognition. It can identify fresh produce, packaged goods, and even distinguish between similar items like different types of peppers or greens.

Do I need to be a good cook to use CookCam?

Not at all! CookCam adapts to your skill level. Beginners get simple, step-by-step recipes while experienced cooks can explore advanced techniques. You set your comfort level.

What if I have dietary restrictions?

CookCam supports all major dietary preferences including vegan, vegetarian, keto, paleo, gluten-free, and more. It also handles allergies and automatically excludes ingredients you can't eat.
